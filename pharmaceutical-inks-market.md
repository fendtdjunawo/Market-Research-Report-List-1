<p><strong>The Pharmaceutical Inks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-based Pharmaceutical Inks</li><li>Solvent-based Pharmaceutical Inks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pharmaceutical inks market is primarily divided into two types: water-based and solvent-based inks. Water-based pharmaceutical inks are composed of water as the main solvent, offering advantages such as low toxicity and environmental friendliness, making them suitable for most pharmaceutical applications. Conversely, solvent-based pharmaceutical inks use organic solvents, providing faster drying times and superior adhesion on various surfaces. Each type has specific applications depending on desired properties, regulatory requirements, and compatibility with manufacturing processes in the pharmaceutical industry.</p></p>
