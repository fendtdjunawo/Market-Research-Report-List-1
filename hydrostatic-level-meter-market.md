<p><ul><li>Side-mount Type Hydrostatic Level Meter</li><li>Throw-in Type Hydrostatic Level Meter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydrostatic level meter market consists of various types, primarily the side-mount and throw-in types. Side-mount hydrostatic level meters are designed for installation on the side of tanks, providing continuous level measurements without intruding into the tank space. In contrast, throw-in hydrostatic level meters are submerged directly into the liquid to measure the pressure and calculate the level. Both types are essential for accurate liquid inventory management in industries like water treatment, oil and gas, and chemicals.</p></p>
