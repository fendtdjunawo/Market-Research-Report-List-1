<p><p>The hosting infrastructure service market comprises three primary types: IaaS (Infrastructure as a Service), PaaS (Platform as a Service), and SaaS (Software as a Service). IaaS provides virtualized computing resources over the internet, enabling users to manage infrastructure without physical hardware. PaaS offers a platform for developers to build, test, and deploy applications without worrying about the underlying infrastructure. SaaS delivers software applications via the internet, allowing users to access them on-demand without installation or maintenance responsibilities.</p></p>
