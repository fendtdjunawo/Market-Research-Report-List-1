Silicon carbide ceramics are widely used in high-temperature and abrasive environments, often in industries such as aerospace and automotive. Silicon nitride ceramics, on the other hand, are valued for their toughness and reliability, making them suitable for applications in cutting tools and electronic components. Both types are pivotal in advancing modern engineering and technology.</p></p>
