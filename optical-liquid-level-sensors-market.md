<p><ul><li>Digital Optical Liquid Level Sensors</li><li>Analog Optical Liquid Level Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical liquid level sensors can be categorized into two main types: digital and analog sensors. Digital optical liquid level sensors provide a binary output, indicating the presence or absence of liquid, facilitating straightforward integration into automation systems. In contrast, analog optical liquid level sensors deliver continuous output, reflecting varying liquid levels, which allows for precise monitoring. Both types leverage light transmission or reflection principles to detect levels, offering advantages in efficiency, reliability, and suitability for diverse applications across industries.</p></p>
