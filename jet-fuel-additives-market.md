<p><p>The jet fuel additives market encompasses various types that enhance fuel performance and safety. Icing inhibitors prevent ice formation in fuel systems, while antioxidants extend the fuel's shelf life by preventing oxidation. Antiknock additives improve engine performance by reducing knocking, while metal deactivators mitigate the effects of metal contaminants. Conductivity improvers enhance static electricity dissipation, and corrosion inhibitors protect fuel systems from degradation. Together, these additives ensure efficient, safe, and reliable operation of aircraft engines.</p></p>
