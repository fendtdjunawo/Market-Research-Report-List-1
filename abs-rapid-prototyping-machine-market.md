<p><ul><li>FDM Technology</li><li>MEM Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ABS Rapid Prototyping Machine market is primarily segmented into two technologies: Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM) and Material Extrusion Modeling (MEM). FDM technology involves the layer-by-layer melting and extrusion of thermoplastic material to create prototypes, offering versatility and ease of use. In contrast, MEM technology utilizes a broader range of materials by selectively dispensed materials, enabling complex geometries and enhanced material properties. Both technologies cater to diverse industries, driving innovation and efficiency in product development processes.</p></p>
