<p><ul><li>Single Belt Groove Drive</li><li>Multiple Belt Grooves Drive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive pulley market comprises two main types: single belt groove drives and multiple belt grooves drives. Single belt groove drives use one groove to transmit power from the engine to various components, making them simpler and cost-effective for smaller engines. In contrast, multiple belt grooves drives feature several grooves, allowing for the operation of multiple accessories simultaneously. This design enhances efficiency and flexibility, catering to more complex systems in modern vehicles that require synchronized operation of various components.</p></p>
