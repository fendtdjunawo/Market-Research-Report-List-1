<p><ul><li>Multiple-order Wave Plate</li><li>Zero-order Wave Plate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wave plates are optical devices used to manipulate the polarization of light. The market comprises two main types: zero-order wave plates and multiple-order wave plates. Zero-order wave plates consist of a single birefringent material, providing minimal optical path length variations and excellent performance for various applications. In contrast, multiple-order wave plates are made from multiple layers of birefringent materials, which enhance efficiency but can introduce more complexity in wavelength sensitivity. Both types are essential in fields like telecommunications, imaging, and laser technology.</p></p>
