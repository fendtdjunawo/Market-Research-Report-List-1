<p><ul><li>Blind Hole Method</li><li>Ring Core Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Stress Gauge market is divided into two primary types: the Blind Hole Method and the Ring Core Method. The Blind Hole Method involves inserting a gauge into a pre-drilled hole, allowing for stress measurement in confined spaces. In contrast, the Ring Core Method removes a cylindrical section of material, providing an accurate assessment of stress distribution around the core. Both methods are essential for evaluating material integrity and performance in various engineering and construction applications, aiding in preventive maintenance and safety assessments.</p></p>
