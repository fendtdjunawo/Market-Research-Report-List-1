<p><p>The vaccine market for non-infectious meningitis includes various types:  </p><p>1. **Polysaccharide Vaccines** target specific bacterial strains, providing short-term immunity.</p><p>2. **Conjugate Vaccines** enhance immune response by linking polysaccharide antigens to proteins, ensuring longer-lasting protection.</p><p>3. **Combination Vaccines** combine multiple antigens to protect against various meningitis-causing organisms in a single shot.</p><p>4. **Men B Vaccines** specifically target Neisseria meningitidis serogroup B, addressing a significant cause of bacterial meningitis, particularly in children and adolescents.</p></p>
