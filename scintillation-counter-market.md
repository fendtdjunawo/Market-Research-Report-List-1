<p><p>The scintillation counter market is categorized into three primary types: organic crystals, inorganic crystals, and polymer phosphors. Organic crystals are known for their high light yield and fast response times, making them suitable for various applications in radiation detection. Inorganic crystals, such as sodium iodide and cesium iodide, offer excellent energy resolution and thermal stability, ideal for high-energy photon detection. Polymer phosphors, being flexible and lightweight, are emerging for portable applications, enhancing the versatility of scintillation counters across different sectors.</p></p>
