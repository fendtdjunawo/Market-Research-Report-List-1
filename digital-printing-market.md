<p><strong>The Digital Printing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Aqueous</li><li>Solvent</li><li>UV-curable</li><li>Latex</li><li>Dye Sublimation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital printing encompasses various market types, each suited for specific applications. Aqueous printing uses water-based inks, ideal for indoor graphics and photos. Solvent printing employs organic solvents, perfect for outdoor signage due to its durability. UV-curable printing utilizes ultraviolet light to cure inks instantly, allowing for vibrant prints on diverse substrates. Latex printing uses water-based inks with latex polymers, combining eco-friendliness and flexibility. Dye sublimation involves converting dye into gas, excellent for fabric and apparel printing, ensuring vivid colors and durability.</p></p>
