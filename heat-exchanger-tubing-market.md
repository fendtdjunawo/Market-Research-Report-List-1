<p><ul><li>Seamless Tube</li><li>Welded Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Heat exchanger tubing is categorized into two main types: seamless tubes and welded tubes. Seamless tubes are manufactured from a solid round billet that is pushed or pulled through a die to create cylindrical tubes without seams, offering enhanced strength and resistance to pressure. In contrast, welded tubes are produced by rolling and welding flat strips of metal, making them more cost-effective for larger projects. Both types are vital in various applications, including oil and gas, chemical processing, and HVAC systems.</p></p>
