<p><strong>The Commercial Jet Engine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low-Pressure Turbine</li><li>High-Pressure Turbine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial jet engine market is segmented into low-pressure turbines (LPT) and high-pressure turbines (HPT). Low-pressure turbines are designed to extract energy from exhaust gases and are typically positioned downstream in the engine, enhancing efficiency and thrust. High-pressure turbines, located upstream, are responsible for driving the engine’s compressor, operating at higher temperatures and pressures to maximize performance. Both types are crucial for optimizing engine efficiency, performance, and overall aircraft operational capabilities in the aviation industry.</p></p>
