<p><p>The Process Photometers market is segmented into three main types: absorbance photometers, turbidity photometers, and others. Absorbance photometers measure the amount of light absorbed by a sample, useful for determining concentrations of substances. Turbidity photometers assess the cloudiness or haziness in liquids, indicating the presence of suspended particles. The "others" category encompasses various specialized photometers used in specific applications, such as fluorescence and reflectance measurements, catering to diverse industrial and laboratory needs.</p></p>
