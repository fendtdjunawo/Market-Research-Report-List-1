<p><p>The CT scan devices market is categorized into three main types based on slicing capabilities: low-slice scanners, medium-slice scanners, and high-slice scanners. Low-slice scanners typically offer 1 to 4 slices per rotation, suitable for basic imaging needs. Medium-slice scanners, ranging from 4 to 16 slices, provide enhanced detail for improved diagnostics. High-slice scanners, with 16 slices and above, deliver rapid imaging and detailed 3D reconstructions, making them ideal for complex cases and advanced medical applications. Each type caters to varying clinical requirements and budgets.</p></p>
