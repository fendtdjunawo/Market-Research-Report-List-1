Hemodialysis involves using a machine to filter blood, while peritoneal dialysis utilizes the body's peritoneal lining for waste removal, offering flexibility. Kidney transplantation is the definitive treatment, replacing the failing organ with a healthy one, significantly improving patients' quality of life and outcomes. Each treatment option targets specific patient needs.</p></p>
