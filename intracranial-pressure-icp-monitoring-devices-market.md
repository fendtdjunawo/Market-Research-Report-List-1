<p><p>The Intracranial Pressure (ICP) monitoring devices market comprises two main types: invasive and non-invasive ICP devices. Invasive ICP devices involve procedures like the insertion of a catheter into the skull, offering accurate and continuous pressure measurements, often used in critical care settings. Non-invasive ICP devices utilize techniques such as transcranial Doppler or magnetic resonance imaging, providing safer alternatives without surgical intervention, suitable for monitoring patients at lower risk or those unable to undergo invasive procedures.</p></p>
