<p><p>Micro Combined Heat and Power (MCHP) systems are categorized into two primary market types: engine-based and fuel-cell based. Engine-based MCHP systems utilize internal combustion engines to generate electricity and produce heat, making them suitable for residential and commercial applications. In contrast, fuel-cell based MCHP systems employ electrochemical processes to convert fuel into electricity, with high efficiency and lower emissions. Both technologies aim to provide efficient energy solutions, but they differ in their operational mechanisms and environmental impacts.</p></p>
