<p><ul><li>Laser Phosphor Display (LPD) Technology</li><li>RGB & RG Lasers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laser display technology market encompasses various types, primarily focused on Laser Phosphor Display (LPD) and RGB & RG lasers. LPD technology combines laser light with phosphors to create vibrant images, ideal for large screens and commercial applications. RGB lasers utilize red, green, and blue lasers for direct color generation, offering superior brightness and color accuracy, often used in high-end projectors. RG lasers, featuring a similar configuration but omitting blue, are emerging for specific applications requiring different color balances.</p></p>
