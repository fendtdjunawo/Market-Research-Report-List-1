<p><p>The Paddy and Rice Milling Machinery market features various types of milling technologies, mainly categorized into horizontal and vertical mill rollers. Horizontal mill rollers utilize a flat grinding surface, offering efficient processing for larger volumes of paddy. In contrast, vertical type mill rollers leverage gravity to enhance grain flow and minimize damage, making them ideal for smaller operations. Both types serve distinct needs within rice milling, balancing efficiency, capacity, and the desired quality of the finished product.</p></p>
