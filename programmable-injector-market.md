<p><p>The programmable injector market is categorized into split and splitless types based on their operation in analytical applications. Split injectors allow a portion of the sample to be introduced into the chromatographic system while venting excess, ideal for concentrated samples. In contrast, splitless injectors introduce the entire sample, making them suitable for trace analysis where higher sensitivity is required. Each type serves distinct analytical needs, optimizing sample introduction for improved resolution and detection in chemical analysis.</p></p>
