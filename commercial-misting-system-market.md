<p><p>The commercial misting system market comprises three main types: low pressure, medium pressure, and high pressure. Low pressure systems operate at around 10-40 psi, providing basic cooling and humidity control. Medium pressure systems, operating between 100-300 psi, offer enhanced mist dispersal and cooling effects, suitable for medium-sized areas. High pressure systems, functioning at 800-1000 psi, create ultra-fine mist that evaporates quickly, delivering superior cooling and efficient moisture control for larger commercial spaces. Each type caters to specific cooling needs.</p></p>
