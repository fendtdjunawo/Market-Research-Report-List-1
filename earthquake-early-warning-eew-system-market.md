<p><p>The Earthquake Early Warning (EEW) System market comprises three key components: earthquake sensors, servers, and receivers. Earthquake sensors detect seismic activity, providing real-time data to identify tremors. Servers process and analyze this data, determining the severity and potential impact of an earthquake. Receivers, which can be installed in homes, businesses, and public institutions, alert users to impending earthquakes, enabling them to take protective measures. Together, these elements form a comprehensive system aimed at minimizing damage and enhancing safety during seismic events.</p></p>
