<p><ul><li>Primary Fatty Amine</li><li>Secondary Fatty Amine</li><li>Tertiary Fatty Amine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fatty amine market is categorized into three main types: primary, secondary, and tertiary fatty amines. Primary fatty amines contain one alkyl group and one amino group, making them effective surfactants and emulsifiers. Secondary fatty amines have two alkyl groups and offer enhanced stability and performance in various applications, including agrochemicals and personal care products. Tertiary fatty amines, with three alkyl groups, are versatile and commonly used in the production of quaternary ammonium compounds and as corrosion inhibitors.</p></p>
