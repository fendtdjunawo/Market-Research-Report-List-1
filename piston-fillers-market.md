<p><p>Piston fillers are categorized into fully-automatic and semi-automatic market types based on their operation and efficiency. Fully-automatic piston fillers operate without human intervention, streamlining production processes for high-volume applications, and are ideal for large-scale manufacturers seeking efficiency and speed.
