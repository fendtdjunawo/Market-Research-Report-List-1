<p><p>The EMI suppression polypropylene film capacitor market is primarily categorized into Class X and Class Y capacitors. Class X capacitors are designed for use across AC lines, serving to mitigate electromagnetic interference and ensuring safety during voltage surges. They are typically utilized in applications like power supplies and motor drives. Class Y capacitors, on the other hand, are intended to connect between the line and ground, focusing on safety by preventing electrical shock. These capacitors are essential for compliance with safety standards in various electronic devices.</p></p>
