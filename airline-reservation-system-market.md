<p><p>The Airline Reservation System (ARS) market can be categorized into two primary types: On-Premise and Web-Based systems. On-Premise systems are installed locally on airline servers, offering complete control and customization but requiring substantial upfront investment and maintenance. In contrast, Web-Based systems are hosted on the cloud, allowing for easier scalability, accessibility from anywhere, and lower initial costs. Each market type caters to different operational needs and budget considerations, influencing airline choices for their reservation technology.</p></p>
