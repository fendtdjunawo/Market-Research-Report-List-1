<p><p>The Very High Bond (VHB) double-sided tapes market can be segmented into three main types: opaque, translucent, and clear double-sided tapes. Opaque double-sided tapes offer excellent adhesion and conceal surfaces, making them ideal for applications where aesthetics are a concern. Translucent double-sided tapes allow some light to pass through, providing a balance between visibility and adherence, suitable for various packaging needs. Clear double-sided tapes provide a nearly invisible bond, making them perfect for applications where transparency is essential, such as in crafting and display mounting.</p></p>
