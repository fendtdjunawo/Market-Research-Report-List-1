<p><strong>The MRSA Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Immunodiagnostics</li><li>Molecular Diagnostics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MRSA testing market is divided into two primary types: immunodiagnostics and molecular diagnostics. Immunodiagnostics utilize antigen-antibody interactions to detect MRSA infections, offering rapid results through techniques like ELISA and lateral flow assays. In contrast, molecular diagnostics focus on identifying MRSA’s genetic material, using methods such as PCR for high sensitivity and specificity. Both approaches are critical for timely diagnosis, guiding effective treatment, and controlling the spread of infection, catering to the needs of healthcare facilities and laboratories.</p></p>
