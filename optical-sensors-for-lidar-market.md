<p><strong>The Optical Sensors For Lidar Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Scanning LiDAR</li><li>Scanning LiDAR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical sensors for LiDAR are categorized into two main types: Non-Scanning LiDAR and Scanning LiDAR. Non-Scanning LiDAR employs a fixed setup that captures data in a specific area without moving parts, making it ideal for applications like close-range mapping. In contrast, Scanning LiDAR utilizes rotating or oscillating mechanisms to cover larger areas, providing detailed 3D mapping over extensive landscapes. Both types serve various industries, including autonomous vehicles, agriculture, and environmental monitoring, enhancing data collection and analysis capabilities.</p></p>
