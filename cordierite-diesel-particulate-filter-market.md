<p><p>The Cordierite Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) market is segmented into active and passive types. Active DPFs utilize external energy sources or additives to enhance regeneration, effectively clearing trapped soot. They are often employed in heavy-duty applications where higher exhaust temperatures are present. Conversely, passive DPFs rely on the natural exhaust heat for regeneration, making them suitable for lighter vehicles with lower operational temperatures. This distinction allows manufacturers to tailor solutions according to specific vehicle requirements and emissions regulations.</p></p>
