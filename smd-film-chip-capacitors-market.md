<p><p>SMD film chip capacitors are categorized by the film materials used in their construction, impacting performance and applications. PPS (Polyphenylene Sulfide) film capacitors offer high thermal stability and resistance to moisture, making them suitable for harsh environments. PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) film capacitors feature good electrical properties and are widely used due to their cost-effectiveness. PEN (Polyethylene Naphthalate) film capacitors provide enhanced thermal and electrical performance, ideal for high-frequency applications. Other types may include specialized or hybrid films catering to specific needs.</p></p>
